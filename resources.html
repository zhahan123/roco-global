🗺️ Top 7 Farming Routes

🥇 #1 Snow Mountain Route

Stormpeak + Starfrost Cliff

Most resource-dense route. Farm Angel Grass, Snow Mushroom, Starfrost Flower, Flame Flower in one loop. Priority #1.

⚡ ~15 min per loop

🥈 #2 Salvage Port Route

Red + Blue dual lines

Farm Sea Hibiscus, Star Horn Flowers plus dense ore nodes. Best for hybrid ore+flower farming.

⚡ ~10 min per loop

🥉 #3 Chirping Mesa Route

High-density cluster

Massive Jet Mushroom, Stone Ear, Snow Mushroom in a compact area. Best time-to-resource ratio.

⚡ ~8 min per loop

#4 Wind Barrier + Sanctum

Meowmeow Grass + Jet Mushroom combo. Can chain together for extended farming sessions.

#5 Market Street → Academy → Mage Home

Urban route: dense ores, Sunflowers, Poseidon Flowers. Great for quick daily resets.

#6 Backfill Canyon Route

Dual sub-routes with dense mineral + flower nodes. Good alternative when main routes are crowded.

#7 Shipwreck Town Route

Flowers + ores mix. Open-pit mine for deep mining. Good for Yellow Garnet stockpiling.

  • • Unlock teleport points first — saves hours
  • • Full mount + share pass yields ~3500-3800 ore
  • • Visit Holyfeather Hall at night for +120 ghost fire
  • • Ghost fire respawns every 30 min

  • • Always carry 50+ empty balls before farming
